Buyer's Notes — Authenticity Tips

Riveted vs. Butted Mail

For a warrior of Somerled's rank, only riveted mail is appropriate. Butted mail is a modern shortcut — save money elsewhere, not here.

Steel Grade

Look for 1075, 1080, or 5160 high-carbon steel in blades. Mild steel is fine for armour and display pieces.

Avoid

Fantasy embellishments (dragon scales, horns, unrealistic spikes), stainless steel blades (wrong period sheen), double-bit axes, cruciform crossguards that are too late-period.

Recommended Vendors

Windlass Steelcrafts and Hanwei for mid-budget weapons. Lord of Battles for mail. Commission Etsy smiths for small blades and knives.

Purchase Order

Gambeson first (sized to your body) → Haubergeon (sized over gambeson) → Helm → Weapons → Blades.

Total Kit Note

A fully riveted, period-correct kit runs $1,400–$2,500+. Cutting corners on mail is the main compromise most reenactors make.
